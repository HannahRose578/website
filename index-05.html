Our Goals

Conel Creative Hub seeks to make art accessible to everyone.

This has been our mission since day 1. Giving students the opportunity and skills to expand artistically and set their foot into the industry. We teach practical skills and let our students experiment and submit their own work whatever they choose.

  • Music Production

  • Games and Animation
